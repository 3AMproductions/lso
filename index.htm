welcome home

Libertarianism, or classical-liberalism, is a belief system where liberty and freedom are considered to be highly desirable qualities of a society's institutions. Because the Libertarian Studies Organization (LSO) is a group devoted to the study of libertarianism, being a member does not presuppose any political or ideological position. Rather, we are an association of thinkers who gather together to hear speakers, have discussions and hold social functions, all organized around the theme of exploring the question how much freedom should people have? All are welcome to attend LSO functions and to offer input in the planning of events.

The Libertarian Studies Organization at The Ohio State University is just one chapter of the Libertarian Studies Organization. The larger organization can found at www.libertarianstudies.net

You may ask for more information or join our mailing list by contacting us at: lso_osu@yahoo.com

"Nolan's Run" is a new documentary about Gary Nolan's run for the Libertarian nomination for president in 2004. It deals with the difficulties of being a third party candidate. We are lucky enough to not only have a special screening of the film, and to have Gary Nolan himself introduce the film, but to also have a special guest appearance by the Libertarian Party's candidate for Governor of Ohio: Bill Peirce.
